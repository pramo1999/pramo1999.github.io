## Overview

This course introduced practical mortality modelling techniques with a focus on cohort and health-informed models. It covered data cleaning, model selection, and communicating results to non-technical audiences.

## Topics Covered

- Introduction to mortality tables
- Cohort-based adjustments
- Model validation and forecasting

## Specialties

- National-scale health data integration
- Survival analysis and state-based models
