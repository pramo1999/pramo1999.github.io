This ongoing learning resource provided by the Actuaries Institute focuses on the critical intersection of climate change, environmental sustainability, and actuarial science. The syllabus covers methodologies for evaluating climate risks, modeling the financial impacts of environmental shifts, and developing sustainable strategies for long-term planning. It equips professionals with the necessary knowledge to integrate climate-related data into core actuarial workflows.
